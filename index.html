Pocket Scammers

Pocket Scammers is a singleplayer 3D turn-based rpg that I led and made with my team of 4 for a studio project at NYP. Players can gain money and exp upon defeating a scammer. The money can then be spent on shops to buy combat skills. Combat skills are what you use to fight. A player can only have 4 skills equipped at each time. I did the shops, implementation of the skills, inventory, scene-switching and the first level design.

Unity bomberman

This is a budget replica of bomberman that I made with Unity. It is a 2 player game where players have to bomb the other to win. There are powerups for players to pick up to increase the radius of the explosion

Mobyland

Mobyland is a singleplayer 2D survival rpg game that I did with a team of 4 for a studio project at NYP. Gather resources and survive. Craft equipments to aid your journey. Find the hidden dungeon and slay the boss to win. Everything of the monsters are done by me. (The spawning, AI, stats, attacks, drops and buffs)
